Time-Series ML · Personal Research

Cycle-Phase & Ovulation Prediction from Wearables

An end-to-end privacy-preserving platform collecting longitudinal HRV, resting HR, sleep, stress and training-load data from multiple participants, with a multi-class time-series pipeline to model cycle phases and detect ovulation from continuous biosignals.

  • FastAPI web app + packaged cross-platform desktop app that gathers wearable data without ever storing credentials
  • Rolling-window feature engineering — lag features, past-only rolling means, day-to-day deltas across HRV, sleep stages & load
  • Statistically validated the follicular→luteal (ovulation) transition is detectable from resting HR & HRV (large effect sizes)

Key result · ovulation detection

Averaged across 27 cycles and aligned to the estimated ovulation day (green line): resting HR steps up after ovulation while HRV drops down — a clean, statistically robust physiological signature. Click to enlarge.
